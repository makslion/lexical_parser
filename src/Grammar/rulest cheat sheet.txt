English Lexical Categories
v verb
n noun
adj adjective
adv adverb
p preposition
det determiner
cor co-ordinator/conjunction
subord subordinator/complementiser
e nothing

Phrasal Categories
S  sentence (used for parser)
NP noun phrase
VP verb phrase
PP prepositional phrase
AP adjectival phrase
ADVP adverbial phrase
QP quantifier phrase

rules are written down as a context free grammar.
For example rule "S -> NP VP" will be written as "S NP VP"
Rules don't support "|" symbol so "NP -> n | det n" should be written as two separate rules "NP n" and "NP det n"
